About Dropshipping

Dropshipping is a retail method where

an online store doesn’t keep its

products in stock. Instead, when a

customer makes an order, the store

forwards it with payment to a

dropshipping supplier.

Dropshipping Working Method

Customer places order & pay you retail price (₹100)

Forward order to your supplier & pay you wholesale price (₹50)

Keeps Profit (₹50)
